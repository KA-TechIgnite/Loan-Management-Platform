Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Loan Accelerator is a comprehensive loan management system that helps you apply for loans, track your application status, calculate EMIs, and manage your loan portfolio all in one place.

You can check your loan eligibility by clicking on the "Eligibility Score" section on your dashboard. Our system will analyze your credit score, income, and other factors to determine your eligibility.

Loan Application

You'll need: Valid ID proof, Address proof, Income statements (salary slips/bank statements), Employment verification, and any collateral documents if applicable.

Pre-approved loans can be sanctioned instantly with no documentation. Regular loan applications typically take 2-7 business days for approval, depending on the loan type and documentation verification.

EMI & Repayment

EMI is calculated using the formula: EMI = [P x R x (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N-1], where P is principal, R is monthly interest rate, and N is tenure in months. Use our EMI Calculator for instant calculations.

Yes, you can make partial or full prepayments. Some loans may have prepayment charges. Check your loan terms or contact support for specific details about your loan.
